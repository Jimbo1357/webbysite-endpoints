Fieldbus Sniffer

Currently in development

Our portable fieldbus sniffer provides direct access to MODBUS, PROFIBUS, and PROFINET systems. With it, you can effortlessly view Device IDs, Function Codes, and Registers in real-time. This tool is invaluable for rapid diagnostics, instantly highlighting communication errors such as unresponsive devices, data conflicts, or other detected issues. Save on time, subcontractor costs and streamline your operations.
